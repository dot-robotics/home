Bannari Amman Institute of Technology
Bannari Amman Institute of Technology, often referred to as BIT, is an esteemed engineering institution located in Sathyamangalam, Tamil Nadu, India. Established in 1996, it is known for its commitment to academic excellence and innovation in the field of technical education.
Manufacturing & Fabrication Lab
Manufacturing & Fabrication Lab at Bannari Amman Institute of Technology is a cutting-edge facility specially dedicated to the research, development, and manufacturing of vehicles and robotics. This specialized lab plays a pivotal role in fostering innovation, enhancing engineering skills, and supporting product development and research in these domains.
