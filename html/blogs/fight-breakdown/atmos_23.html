#Fight Breakdown | ATMOS'23

Navigating Challenges and Celebrating Team Spirit: Our Atmos'23 Combat Robot Experience

Published on December 30, 2023

The Atmos'23 Combat Robot event proved to be a thrilling and challenging experience for our team. As we geared up for the competition, little did we know that the pre-match preparations would put our skills to the test. In this blog post, we'll delve into the hurdles we faced in drive and weapon assembly and express our gratitude to the incredible support we received from DS Robotics, Team Fury, Team Vulcan, and the event coordinators.

Pre-Match Challenge

As any robotics enthusiast knows, the road to a competition is paved with hours of meticulous preparation. However, despite our best efforts, we encountered unexpected issues in both drive and weapon assembly during the crucial pre-match phase. It was a race against time to troubleshoot and fine-tune our robot for optimal performance on the battlefield.

Supportive Community

In moments of crisis, it's the strength of the robotics community that truly shines. We are immensely grateful for the unwavering support extended to us by DS Robotics, Team Fury, and Team Vulcan. Their expertise, camaraderie, and willingness to lend a helping hand played a pivotal role in overcoming the challenges we faced. It's moments like these that reinforce the sense of unity within the robotics community, where competitors become allies in the pursuit of shared passion and excellence.

Match 1: Loki vs. Vulcan – The Clash of Titans

The opening match set the stage for a fierce showdown between Loki and the formidable Vulcan. Both bots brought their A-game, exchanging blows and tactical maneuvers. In a stunning turn of events, Loki managed to secure victory with a tap-out from Vulcan, showcasing the prowess of our meticulously designed combat robot.

Match 2: Unforeseen Hurdles Against ThriveForce

As the competition progressed, we faced unforeseen technical issues in our match against ThriveForce. Despite the setbacks, our team demonstrated resilience and sportsmanship. Technical glitches are an inherent part of robotics, and our decision to prioritize safety and fairness ultimately led to a withdrawal from the match.

The Journey to the Finals

With a mix of triumphs and challenges, we found ourselves in the finals against Rowdy from DS Robotics. The atmosphere was tense, but there was an underlying camaraderie between competitors who share a passion for the world of combat robotics. DS and DOT, though friends off the arena, were determined to give the audience a match to remember.

Finals Face-Off: Loki vs. Rowdy

In a battle that pushed the limits of both bots, Loki and Rowdy showcased the culmination of months of hard work and dedication. The crowd was on the edge of their seats as sparks flew and metal clashed. It was a tight competition, a true test of skill and strategy. In the end, while Rowdy emerged victorious, the spirit of friendly competition prevailed, leaving both teams with a deep sense of respect for each other's achievements.

Shoutout to DS Robotics, Team Fury, and Team Vulcan

DS Robotics, Team Fury, and Team Vulcan exemplify the true spirit of sportsmanship and collaboration. Their technical know-how, encouragement, and collaborative problem-solving not only helped us address the issues at hand but also strengthened the bonds within the robotics community. A heartfelt thank you to these amazing teams for embodying the essence of teamwork and support.

Conclusion

The Atmos'23 Robotics Competition was not just a test of machines but a celebration of the passion and innovation that drives the world of combat robotics. As we reflect on our journey – the victories, challenges, and friendships forged in the heat of battle – we look forward to future competitions and the continued evolution of our combat bot, Loki. The lessons learned and the memories created at Atmos'23 will undoubtedly fuel our determination for the challenges that lie ahead in the exciting world of competitive robotics.
