Vedha v1 | THE BEST ONE YET.

Overview

Vedha, the agile 25kg Robot Sumo bot, defies expectations with its compact size and unmatched winning streak. Sporting a wedge-type design, Vedha strategically leverages its smaller form to outmaneuver opponents in the arena. Despite its size disadvantage, consistent triumphs showcase its exceptional agility, precise control, and strategic prowess. With an impressive winning streak, stands as a testament to the effectiveness of its design and the skillful execution of its tactical approach in the competitive realm of Robot Sumo.

Drive

Drive system is powered by an E-Bike motor coupled with a 9:1 gearbox, delivering a potent combination of speed and torque. The Mobitronix 60D ESC controls and optimizes the motor's performance, ensuring seamless maneuverability and responsiveness in the heat of battle. This dynamic setup allows the bot to swiftly navigate the arena, utilizing the advantages of its wedge design to outmaneuver opponents and maintain its winning streak in the competitive landscape of Robot Sumo.

Base

Robust chassis is constructed from 5mm thick Mild Steel (MS), providing a sturdy foundation for its agile and strategic maneuvers in the Robot Sumo arena. The durable 5mm MS chassis not only contributes to the bot's overall structural integrity but also enhances its resilience against the intense physical demands of competition. This thoughtful choice in chassis material underscores commitment to durability and stability, key factors that contribute to its continued success on the sumo bot battlefield.

Gameplay

Gameplay strategy centers around its wedge design, aiming to dominate the arena by leveraging its agility and strength. With precise control and strategic movements, employs its formidable wedge to push opponents outside the arena boundaries, capitalizing on its size and exceptional maneuverability. While contending with opponents equipped with flippers or wedges, skillful navigation and strategic evasions become crucial. This combination is making it a formidable force in the competitive world of Robot Sumo.

serviceability

Serviceability is a key advantage, facilitated by its streamlined design with fewer components. This intentional simplicity not only enhances the robot's efficiency in the heat of battle but also ensures ease of repair post-matches. With spare parts readily available and a straightforward design, bot can be quickly and easily repaired, minimizing downtime and maximizing its readiness for subsequent rounds. This focus on serviceability reflects commitment to maintaining peak performance throughout the competitive landscape of Robot Sumo.
