THEERA V3 | THE BEST ONE YET.

Overview

New Techfest? New Theera. We've showcased a revamped version of Theera at every Techfest, pushing the boundaries each year. Our focus is on enhancing performance, integrating cutting-edge features, leveraging the latest technology, and, naturally, ensuring it looks as impressive as ever. Where's the fun without some signature Theera style? This year is no exception, as we unveil a completely redesigned bot from the ground up. Stay tuned for an awe-inspiring performance as Theera takes center stage in the relentless arena of competition.

Drive

For our latest drive, we've opted for the reliable combination of Short Ampflow motors paired with a custom 8:1 gearbox, and the Tekin RX8 Gen3 serving as the ESC. These components have proven themselves to be well-suited for our requirements. While some may suggest a brushless drive setup, we've found that our current configuration more than meets our needs. However, stay tuned for the upcoming season, as we're already in the planning stages for a transition to a Brushless Drive Setup, aiming to take our performance to the next level.

Chassis

Opting for a modular approach, our chassis design incorporates replaceable side plates, rear plates, and top plates, providing easy maintenance in case of damage. The chassis material of choice is robust 16mm aluminum, striking a balance between durability and weight efficiency. This design ensures flexibility and swift repairs, allowing us to stay in top form throughout the competition. We believe in adaptability, and this modular chassis design aligns perfectly with our commitment to pushing the limits of innovation.

Armour

In the quest for superior protection, our choice for armor includes the formidable AR400, strategically employed as both the wedge and side plate cover. This configuration serves as a formidable defense against the impacts delivered by vertical spinners and drum bots. AR400's exceptional hardness and toughness make it an ideal material, providing the necessary resilience to withstand punishing blows. Our commitment to durability and strategic design ensures that Theera remains a formidable contender.

serviceability

Serviceability is a paramount consideration in Theera's design philosophy. Our modular chassis allows for easy and efficient replacements of key components, such as side plates, rear plates, and top plates, in the event of damage. This modular approach streamlines maintenance and minimizes downtime, ensuring quick turnarounds between matches. This choice not only provides effective protection against impacts from vertical spinners and drum bots but also facilitates straightforward repairs and replacements when needed.
