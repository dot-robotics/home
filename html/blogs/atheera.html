Atheera V2 | THE BEST ONE YET.

Overview

Atheera, a formidable wedge bot in the under 15kg category, redefines the essence of simplicity and effectiveness in combat robotics. With a sleek and purposeful design, Atheera is engineered to dominate the arena with precision and strategic prowess. The wedge configuration ensures a low profile, enabling Atheera to control the battlefield, deflect opponents, and dictate the pace of the match. Compact, agile, and purpose-built, Atheera represents the epitome of efficiency in the relentless world of robotic combat.

Drive

In a continuous quest for enhanced performance, Loki has undergone a remarkable evolution in its drive system. Transitioning from RS775 motors in Version 1 to the upgraded RZ735 motors in Version 2 marked a significant leap. Now, gearing up for even greater power, Loki is making the leap to a brushless setup, driven by D3536 1250KV motors with precision-engineered BaneBots gearbox. This strategic shift promises heightened speed, agility, and overall maneuverability, solidifying Loki's position as a cutting-edge force in the realm of combat robotics.

Chassis

Learning from past challenges, we've meticulously refined Loki's chassis design to ensure optimal performance in every aspect. While Version 1 encountered a flaw in its center of gravity, leading to locomotion issues, Version 2 emerged as a perfected solution. With a precisely engineered chassis, Version 2 eliminated previous concerns and delivered flawless maneuverability. Building upon this success, Version 3 retains the same perfected chassis design, ensuring a seamless fusion of stability and agility in the relentless arena of combat robotics.

Armour

Atheera boasts a formidable defense with its armor crafted from 5mm thick AR500, underscoring its commitment to durability and resilience in the under 15kg category. This strategic choice of armor material provides Atheera with robust protection against a variety of opponents, enhancing its survivability and capacity to withstand the intense impacts of the arena. The 5mm AR500 armor stands as a formidable barrier, ensuring that Atheera remains a stalwart contender in the fast-paced and challenging landscape of combat robotics.

serviceability

We've learned a lot over the years about what works and what doesn't in the pits between matches. To save time and simplify repair on-the-fly, Loki's guts have been modularized.Embraces a serviceability-focused design, featuring a modular architecture that prioritizes ease of maintenance. Our commitment to user-friendly serviceability means that key components are designed for effortless replacement, minimizing downtime and simplifying repairs. Whether it's the chassis, drive system, or weaponry, ensuring that the bot is always in optimal condition for the next battle.
