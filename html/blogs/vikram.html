Vikram v1 | THE BEST ONE YET.

Overview

Vikram, the 25kg Robot Sumo bot, represents a transformative era in the competitive landscape, rewriting history with its dominant performance. Emerging at a crucial juncture when Vedha faced setbacks, Vikram was born to redefine the narrative. Since its inception, Vikram has dominated the Robot Sumo scene, showcasing unparalleled skill, precision, and strategic prowess. This shift in fortune underscores Vikram's remarkable impact on the sport, marking a new chapter of success and resilience in the history of competitive Robot Sumo.

Drive

Drive system is meticulously calibrated for optimal performance, operating at a controlled speed range of 300-350 RPM. Powering this precision is the Tekin RX8 Gen3 ESC, a sophisticated electronic speed controller that ensures smooth and responsive control over the drive motors. This dynamic combination of RPM tuning and advanced ESC technology contributes to the exceptional agility and maneuverability on the Robot Sumo arena, allowing it to maintain its dominance with strategic finesse and precise movements.

Base

Foundation is built on a robust 4mm aluminum base, combining strength and lightweight durability. This carefully chosen material provides the structural integrity needed to withstand the rigors of intense Robot Sumo competition while maintaining agility and responsiveness. The 4mm aluminum base reflects the commitment to a balanced design, ensuring a formidable presence in the arena as it continues to rewrite the history of Robot Sumo with its dominant performance.

Gameplay

Gameplay strategy centers around its wedge design, aiming to dominate the arena by leveraging its agility and strength. With precise control and strategic movements, employs its formidable wedge to push opponents outside the arena boundaries, capitalizing on its size and exceptional maneuverability. While contending with opponents equipped with flippers or wedges, skillful navigation and strategic evasions become crucial. This combination is making it a formidable force in the competitive world of Robot Sumo.

serviceability

Serviceability is a key advantage, facilitated by its streamlined design with fewer components. This intentional simplicity not only enhances the robot's efficiency in the heat of battle but also ensures ease of repair post-matches. With spare parts readily available and a straightforward design, bot can be quickly and easily repaired, minimizing downtime and maximizing its readiness for subsequent rounds. This focus on serviceability reflects commitment to maintaining peak performance throughout the competitive landscape of Robot Sumo.
