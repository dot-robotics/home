#Theera

Overview

Theera is a formidable combat robot designed to compete in the intense 120lb weight category of robotic combat sports. With its sleek and effective wedge design, Theera is engineered to dominate the arena by outmaneuvering and outwitting its opponents. Whether it's in a battle inside a steel cage or on the arena floor, Theera is ready to showcase its power, agility, and strategic prowess.
