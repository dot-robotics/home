Overview

"Atheera," the agile 30-pound combat bot inspired by the mighty "Theera." Named after its larger predecessor, Atheera inherits the essence of Theera's strength and resilience. Like its bigger Brother, With its low-profile wedge shape, Atheera is a formidable force, ready to outmaneuver and outwit its adversaries. Get ready to witness the legacy of Theera live on as Atheera takes on the competition, one strategic push at a time.
