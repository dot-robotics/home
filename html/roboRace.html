How the Robo-Race Works?

"RoboRace is an electrifying competition where robots, whether wired or wireless, race on a track that's as much about technology as it is about speed. In this thrilling event, teams of engineers and programmers put their creations to the test, guiding them manually through a challenging course. It's a true showcase of robotics ingenuity and human skill, where the race is not just about winning but pushing the boundaries of what's possible in the world of autonomous machines."
