Fight 2 [QuantumX'24] | Raptor V2!

WD

January 13, 2024

Pre-Match

In the lead-up to the previous match, Honey Badger underwent a smooth and efficient pre-match preparation. With no significant issues to address, our team conducted thorough checks on the wiring system and meticulously tightened all fasteners to ensure optimal performance. A final touch of charging the batteries completed the routine, leaving us with a bot that was primed and ready for action. This seamless pre-match process exemplified our team's dedication to meticulous preparation and attention to detail, setting the stage for a successful and victorious performance in the arena.

Battle Begins

In the electrifying match between Honey Badger and Raptor V2, our bot weathered the storm of opponent hits with resilience, showcasing its robust design and durability. As the battle unfolded, we strategically utilized the arena space, skillfully creating momentum to seize control. In a crucial moment, a precisely targeted hit on Raptor V2 resulted in a sudden and explosive turn of events – the opponent's battery detonated, marking a spectacular end to the match.

Chassis Image Desc

The strategic escape, precise maneuvering, and well-timed attack demonstrated not only the technical prowess of Honey Badger but also the tactical finesse of our team. This unexpected turn of events served as a testament to the unpredictable nature of combat robotics, where strategy, precision, and a bit of luck can play decisive roles in determining the outcome of a match. As the smoke cleared and the crowd erupted in cheers, the victory against Raptor V2 added another thrilling chapter to Honey Badger's journey in the arena.

Our Appreciation

We extend our heartfelt appreciation to the dedicated team at Terrorbull Robotics for their extraordinary commitment and determination in constructing Raptor V2 under challenging circumstances. Despite the tight deadline and the grueling demands of the competition, the team's unwavering dedication shone through as they worked tirelessly through the night following the qualifiers to bring Raptor V2 to life.

"True dedication knows no bounds in the world of combat robotics. When passion meets perseverance, incredible feats are achieved, even in the darkest hours of the night." — Will Bales, Captain of Team HyperShock

Their selfless dedication, sacrificing proper sleep and personal comfort for the sake of their passion, exemplifies the true spirit of combat robotics. It is a testament to their resilience, perseverance, and unwavering commitment to excellence. The completion of Raptor V2 in the early hours of the morning stands as a remarkable feat of ingenuity, teamwork, and sheer determination.

We commend the members of Terrorbull Robotics for their remarkable achievement and for inspiring us all with their unwavering dedication to their craft. Their dedication serves as a shining example of the passion and determination that drive the combat robotics community forward.
