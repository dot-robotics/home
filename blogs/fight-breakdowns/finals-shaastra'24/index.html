Finals [Shaastra'24] | Arrow & Shunya

WD

January 13, 2024

Pre-Match

In the intense showdown of Shaastra'24 Finals, Honey Badger found itself amidst formidable opponents – Shunya, a formidable Wedge Bot. As the battle unfolded, our team faced an unexpected setback when a post-match inspection revealed issues with the drive motor, rendering it ineffective. With the clock ticking and the pressure mounting, we made a bold decision to switch to a Brushless Drive System featuring the D3536 910KV motor for added power and reliability.

Despite the last-minute change, time was of the essence, and we couldn't have done it alone. Our heartfelt gratitude goes out to Team Uvira for their invaluable assistance in soldering our motors with the ESC, ensuring a seamless transition to the new drive system. Additionally, we extend our sincere appreciation to DS Robotics for their expertise in programming the ESC, optimizing the performance of our bot for the upcoming battle. And last but not least, a special thanks to Team Wild Torquz for their unwavering support in helping us complete the bot in record time.

Battle Begins

As the battle commenced, the arena crackled with tension and anticipation. Shunya's wedge-like design and Honey Badger's formidable Vertical Spinner clashed in a whirlwind of metal and sparks. With each bot showcasing its unique strengths and strategies, the excitement reached fever pitch as the first-ever triple threat match unfolded. Amidst the chaos and intensity, Honey Badger remained steadfast, unleashing devastating blows and maneuvering with precision.

Image Desc

As the dust settled and the cheers of victory echoed through the arena, Honey Badger emerged triumphant in the inaugural triple threat match of Shaastra'24. It was a testament to the resilience, teamwork, and ingenuity of our team, overcoming challenges and emerging victorious against formidable opponents. With this thrilling victory behind us, we look forward to the battles that lie ahead, ready to continue pushing the boundaries of combat robotics and seizing glory in the arena.

"Combat robotics is the ultimate fusion of engineering and adrenaline. It's about pushing the limits of what's possible while facing the unpredictable challenges of the arena." — Grant Imahara
